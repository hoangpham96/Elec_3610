WELCOME TO NETGAME

Introduction about our site

Gamers run the risk of disliking the game in the first few hours and never touching it again. This project aims to bring such subscription services to PC gamers. Despite lucrative opportunities present in console and mobile markets, the scope of this project is limited to only PC gaming. It would offer the customer the ability to discover exciting new indie games or famous classics with a smaller budget. For $15/month, players can play a large collection of games whenever they want and however much they want. The collection will only get bigger and bigger as more games are added to it. Players can cut their monthly gaming budget immensely. With such a low price point, customers can eliminate the feeling of buyer’s remorse and be more willing to expand their gaming interest across different categories. If they are not satisfied with a game, they can just stop playing and search for other games fits their interest. If the customers are not satisfied with the collection of games offered, they can cancel their subscription any time they want. As a bonus, some AAA games may be added to the collection for a limited time the customer can experience the latest and greatest as well.


Business background

With all of our team members being gamers and customers of various digital retail platforms ourselves, we realize that the way gaming as a hobby can deter a lot of people by the price alone. Most people do not have the spare $60-$100 a month to enjoy just one or two games, especially when other form of entertainment already costs them. People who are unfamiliar with gaming are also hesitant to try when a game can cost a week's worth of food. By buying a game, they may face a risk of getting bored within the first 1-2 hours and never playing the game ever again. With the practice of giving out demos going out of style and more and more games are coming out unfinished, unpolished and neglected by the developers, a new game is riskier than ever to buy. NetGame intends to create a new platform to deal exactly with that issue.
